Lights Out - Revenge of Lights Out

How to Play

Click on a square to toggle it and its adjacent squares. Goal: Turn all squares white!

Hint

Plan your moves carefully. Every click affects neighbors!

Font Notes

The board uses a custom font: Comic Sans MS for playful effect.

Addendum

For this project, I implemented a playable Lights Out game using JavaScript. I ensured the board is always solvable by starting with all-white squares and applying random simulated clicks. CSS classes manage square states, and the side panel provides instructions and hints. Challenges included keeping the board responsive and ensuring the “You win!” detection works correctly. Insights gained: solving Lights Out programmatically requires careful consideration of board state and toggling logic.
